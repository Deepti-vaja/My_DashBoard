# Mobile Sales Market Trend & Revenue Dashboard
📊 It is Power BI dashboard that uncovers mobile sales trends, revenue insights, and customer preferences for data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Purpose  
The Mobile Sales Dashboard consolidates raw transactional data into a visually engaging, analytical tool.  
It helps business managers, analysts, and strategists answer critical questions like:  

- Which brands and models are driving revenue?  
- How do sales vary by city, month, or day of the week?  
- What payment methods are most preferred by customers?  
- How does this year’s performance compare to the last?  

---
## 🛠️ Tech Stack  

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:  

- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform  
- 📂 **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and shaping  
- 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Custom measures and calculated fields  
- 📝 **Data Modeling** – Relationships established among transactional and reference tables  
- 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for dashboard development, `.png` for screenshots/previews  

---
## 📂 Data Source  

The dataset used for this dashboard contains **transactional sales records** from a simulated mobile retail business.  

It includes details such as:  
- Transaction ID & Date  
- Mobile Brand & Model  
- Units Sold & Price  
- City & Customer Info  
- Payment Method  

This structured dataset enables analysis of:  
- Sales performance by **brand/model**  
- **City-wise distribution** of sales  
- Customer **payment preferences**  
- **Monthly & yearly sales trends**  

---
## ✨ Features / Highlights  

### 🔎 Business Problem  
Mobile sales data is often scattered across multiple sources, making it difficult for managers to quickly evaluate:  
- Revenue contribution by brand/model  
- Regional sales performance  
- Preferred payment modes  
- Seasonal or yearly fluctuations  

Without a consolidated view, businesses risk missing key opportunities in **marketing, inventory, and pricing strategy**.  

---
### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard  
To deliver a single, interactive Power BI report that:  
- Consolidates mobile sales data into **one source of truth**  
- Provides **real-time insights** into revenue, transactions, and customer behavior  
- Supports **brand, model, and city-level comparisons**  
- Enables **data-driven decisions** for growth and strategy  

---

### 📊 Key Visuals  

- **KPI Cards (Top Section):**  
  - Total Sales: **769M**  
  - Total Quantity: **19K**  
  - Total Transactions: **153.8M**  
  - Avg. Price: **40.11K**  

- **Customer Ratings Panel:** Distribution of feedback into *Good, Average, Bad*.  

- **Sales by City (Map):** Highlights top-performing metros (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad).  

- **Monthly Sales Trends (Line Chart):** Identifies seasonal peaks and slowdowns.  

- **Top Brands/Models (Bar Chart):** Ranks Galaxy Note 20, Galaxy S21, iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, Vivo V51, etc.  

- **Sales by Day of Week (Line/Bar):** Compares weekday vs. weekend performance.  

- **Payment Method Split (Pie Chart):** Shows % split across *Credit Card, Debit Card, Cash, UPI*.  

- **Yearly & Quarterly Comparison (Tables/Bar):** Tracks growth vs. previous year.  

- **Filters (Slicers):** Dynamic filters for *Month, Brand, Model, Payment Method*.  

---

### 🚀 Business Impact & Insights  

- **Revenue Growth Tracking:** Year-over-year performance for strategic planning.  
- **Brand Strategy:** Identify strong vs. weak performers to reallocate marketing budgets.  
- **Geographical Insights:** Pinpoint high-performing cities and target underperforming regions.  
- **Customer Preference Analysis:** Track buying behavior by model, payment type, and time.  
- **Decision Support:** Provides leadership with a consolidated, real-time decision tool.
