   ## Features / Highlights  

### ‚Ä¢ Business Problem  
Mobile sales data is often scattered across multiple sources, making it hard for managers to quickly evaluate:  
- Which brands and models are driving revenue?  
- How sales vary by city, month, or day of the week?  
- What payment methods customers prefer?  
- How current sales compare with last year‚Äôs performance?  

Without a consolidated view, decision-makers risk missing key insights that could optimize marketing, inventory, and pricing strategies.  

---

### ‚Ä¢ Goal of the Dashboard  
To build an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that:  
- Consolidates sales data into a single source of truth.  
- Provides actionable insights on revenue, transactions, and customer behavior.  
- Enables brand-, model-, and city-level comparisons.  
- Supports data-driven decisions in marketing, inventory management, and business growth.  

---

### ‚Ä¢ Walkthrough of Key Visuals  

- **KPI Cards (Top Section):**  
  - Total Sales (769M)  
  - Total Quantity (19K)  
  - Total Transactions (153.8M)  
  - Average Price (40.11K)  

- **Customer Ratings Panel:** Categorizes customer feedback into Good, Average, and Bad.  

- **Sales by City (Map):** Highlights top-performing cities such as Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad.  

- **Monthly Sales Trends (Line Chart):** Shows fluctuations in total sales quantity across months to identify seasonal trends.  

- **Top Brands/Models (Bar Chart):** Ranks popular models like Galaxy Note 20, Galaxy S21, iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, and Vivo V51 by sales contribution.  

- **Sales by Day of Week (Line/Bar Chart):** Compares sales volume across weekdays and weekends.  

- **Payment Method Split (Pie Chart):** Breaks down transactions across Credit Card, Debit Card, Cash, and UPI.  

- **Yearly & Quarterly Comparison (Tables/Bar Chart):** Displays current vs. last year‚Äôs sales for performance benchmarking.  

- **Filters (Slicers):** Dynamic filters for Month, Brand, Model, and Payment Method to support detailed analysis.  

---

### ‚Ä¢ Business Impact & Insights  

- **Revenue Growth Tracking:** Managers can monitor year-over-year performance and identify growth opportunities.  
- **Brand Strategy:** Easily compare brand performance to allocate marketing budgets effectively.  
- **Geographical Insights:** Spot high-performing cities and expand strategies to underperforming regions.  
- **Customer Preference Analysis:** Understand buying patterns by model, payment method, and time period.  
- **Decision Support:** Provides leadership with a single dashboard for real-time, data-driven decisions.
